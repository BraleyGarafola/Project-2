Our Mission

Blue Sea is here to help keep our ocean creates safe. Our jewelry is made from eco-friendly materials and all the proceeds got to the ocean conservation efforts.

About Us

Blue Sea Jewelry started in 2016 when our founder, Lindsey Mckay, heard about how most jewelry makers cause leakage of harmful chemicals into the water supply. That causes the ocean creatures to get sick and eventually die off. After that, Lindsey worked day and night to find eco-friendly ways to make stylish jewelry.
